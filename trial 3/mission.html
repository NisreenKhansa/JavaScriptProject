Our Mission

The Lebanese University is the only public institution in Lebanon that provides higher education in various disciplines, engages in scientific research, and offers continuous training. Our mission encompasses:

  • Dissemination of knowledge and culture.
  • Provision of scientifically qualified human resources.
  • Community service through studies and continuous training to meet the needs of development.
  • Scientific presence at the national, regional, and global levels.
  • Deepening social and national integration.
  • Instilling human values in the hearts of citizens.

Join us in our journey towards advancing knowledge and enriching our community.
